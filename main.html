Age Restrictions
The age of the donor must be above 18 years and below 65 years of age.
Haemoglobin Count
He/she must have a haemoglobin count that is not less than 12.5 g/dl.
Weight Limits
The Weight should not less than 45 kgs.
Body Temperature
One should have a normal body temperature at the time of donation.

Bio Medical Services

Blood Group Serology Human Leukocyte Antigen (HLA) Testing Therapeutic Apheresis Cell and Gene Therapy Solutions
With decades of experience in transfusion medicine and serology, the Red Cross offers the expertise and technology to help you achieve the best possible outcomes for your patients. The NRLBGS assists with the identification of red blood cell (RBC) antibodies, RBC phenotyping and crossmatching, and provides matched RBC units for patients requiring phenotype-selected RBC products in the management of sickle cell disease, as well as antigen-negative and IgA-deficient blood products.
American Red Cross Blood Services is dedicated to providing the safest, most cost-effective blood products and services. Our HLA laboratories provide comprehensive and state-of-the-art HLA services supporting hematopoietic cell transplantation (HCT), Solid Organ Transplantation, Transfusion, Disease/Drug Association Study, Vaccine research, and more.
Red Cross Therapeutic Apheresis Services are helping professionals save lives in your community every day. Nationwide, the American Red Cross performs more than 10,000 therapeutic apheresis procedures per year.
Services offered include:-
Plasmapheresis
Cytapheresis
Photopheresis
LDL Apheresis
Hematopoietic Progenitor Cell (HPC) harves
The Red Cross offers the services and expertise needed to deliver quality cell therapy products, on your timeline, efficiently and consistently, including developing and implementing client-specific collections and processing protocols to meet your unique requirements. As a strong partner to our clients, our team of technical leads and apheresis experts guide your customized solutions and support your advanced therapeutic development from discovery through commercialization.
